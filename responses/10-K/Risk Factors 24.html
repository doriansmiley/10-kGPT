This separation between voting power and economic interest can result in conflicts of interest between the Founders and other stockholders, which could result in actions that are only beneficial to the Founders and not other stockholders. This could lead to a significant and outsized voting power for the Founders and their affiliates, which could be unfavorable for other stockholders. Overall, this information is negative and suggests potential conflicts of interest and unequal voting power.
