Source: https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1321655/000132165522000032/pltr-20220930.htm

Rating: Positive, Score: Above Average Summary: The company operates in the software industry and provides software platforms for institutions to integrate their data, decisions, and operations at scale. The company has built three principal software platforms, Gotham, Foundry, and Apollo. For the three months ended September 30, 2022, the company generated $477.9 million in revenue, reflecting a 22% growth rate from the same period in 2021. The company incurred losses from operations of $62.2 million from July to September 2022, but generated adjusted income from operations of $81.3 million when excluding stock-based compensation and related employer payroll taxes. The company has 337 customers, including companies in various commercial sectors and government agencies around the world, and 56% of their revenue came from government customers, while 44% came from commercial customers. The company expects that U.S. customers will continue to be a significant source of revenue growth. Overall, the company's expansion of access to platforms and partnerships with institutions that have the leadership necessary to effect structural change within their organizations present promising opportunities for growth.
