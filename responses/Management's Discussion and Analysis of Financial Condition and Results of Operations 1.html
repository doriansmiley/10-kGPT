Management's Discussion and Analysis of Financial Condition and Results of Operations 1

Source: https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1321655/000132165522000032/pltr-20220930.htm

Rating: Positive, Score: Above Average Summary: The company has made investments in companies that rely on effective data management, which has enhanced their own product development. While the COVID-19 pandemic has had a negative impact on some customers, the company has seen an increase in adoption of their software due to customers realizing the extended timelines of implementing new software solutions. The Russian invasion of Ukraine has not had a material impact on the company's business and results of operations. The company uses contribution margin to evaluate their business, which is a key measure for their management team. They believe their contribution margin provides an important measure of the efficiency of their operations over time. The company uses non-GAAP measures such as contribution margin, gross profit and gross margin, excluding stock-based compensation, and adjusted income from operations to help them evaluate their business, identify trends affecting their business, formulate business plans and financial projections and make strategic decisions. The company has seen an increase in gross profit and gross margin, excluding stock-based compensation, for the three and nine months ended September 30, 2022, compared to the same period in 2021. The contribution margin percentage has decreased slightly. Overall, the information provided in this filing is positive for a potential investor, but it should be considered in conjunction with other relevant information.
