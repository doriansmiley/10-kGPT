Management's Discussion and Analysis of Financial Condition and Results of Operations 3

Source: https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1321655/000132165522000032/pltr-20220930.htm

Rating: Positive, Score: Above Average The revenue has increased significantly for both the three months and nine months ended September 30, 2022, primarily driven by an increase in revenue from government customers and commercial customers. The increase in revenue from existing customers is a positive sign and is mostly attributed to increased adoption of products and services. However, the gross margin decreased slightly for the three months ended September 30, 2022 due to increased costs, and the increase in cost of revenue for the nine months ended September 30, 2022, has led to a lower than average gross margin for the period. The operating expenses have increased for both periods; a significant increase in sales and marketing related expenses has contributed to the increase in total operating expenses. However, the research and development expenses have decreased for the nine months ended September 30, 2022 compared to the same period in 2021, primarily due to a decrease in stock-based compensation expenses. Overall, the revenue growth is encouraging, and the decrease in research and development expenses could be viewed positively. However, the increase in sales and marketing costs, and the decrease in gross margin is a bit of a concern. Overall, the score is above average, indicating that the company performed better than an average high-growth company during the same period.
