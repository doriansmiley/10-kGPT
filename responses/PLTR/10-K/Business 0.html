Rating: Positive, Score: Above Average The company's software helps organizations integrate their data, decisions, and operations at scale. The software can be ready in days which is preferred by customers who need immediate results over internal software development efforts that may take months or years. The company has built three principal software platforms: Gotham, Foundry, and Apollo which can either be used separately or bundled together. The software is used across more than 60 industries around the world and by many of the world's leading government and commercial institutions. The company earned $1.9 billion in revenue in 2022, with 56% coming from government customers and 44% coming from commercial customers. The business continues to have a global presence, earning 61% of its revenue from customers in the United States, and 39% from those abroad. The company has expanding relationships with existing customers, with the average revenue for its top 20 customers in 2022 being $49.4 million. Despite a history of failures when investing in new technologies, the company's customer acquisition strategy targets large-scale, hard-to-execute opportunities at large government and commercial institutions.
