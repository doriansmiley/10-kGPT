Rating: Positive, Score: Average Summary: The information provided in the extract is positive for potential investors. The company measures fair value based on a three-level hierarchy of inputs, which is considered best practice. Also, the company recognizes revenue upon the transfer of promised goods or services to customers in an amount that reflects the consideration to which the company expects to be entitled in exchange for promised goods or services, in accordance with ASC 606. This method is in line with generally accepted accounting principles. The company recognizes revenue over the contractual term on a ratable basis for Palantir Cloud and On-Premises Software and professional services contracts. There are no significant financing components in the contracts as the payment terms and conditions are generally within one year. There are no material costs for obtaining contracts, costs to fulfill contracts, or software development costs.
