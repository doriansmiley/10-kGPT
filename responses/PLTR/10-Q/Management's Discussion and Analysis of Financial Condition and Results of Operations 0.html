Management's Discussion and Analysis of Financial Condition and Results of Operations 0

Source: https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1321655/000132165522000032/pltr-20220930.htm

Rating: Positive, Score: Strong Summary: - The company provides software platforms that empower organizations to effectively integrate their data, decisions, and operations at scale. - They have built three principal software platforms, Gotham, Foundry, and Apollo, with Foundry becoming a central operating system for individual institutions and entire industries. - The company plans to continue expanding its ability to sell subscriptions globally, investing in resources to address business needs of local markets, including expanding sales and marketing, ecosystem of service partners, and investing in personnel to support growing customer base and product offerings. - The company's focus in the near term is to build partnerships with institutions that have the leadership necessary to effect structural change within their organizations, while over the long term, they believe every institution in their markets is a potential partner. - The company generated $477.9 million in revenue in the three months ended September 30, 2022, reflecting a 22% growth rate from the same period last year. - In the same period, they incurred losses from operations of $62.2 million, or generated adjusted income from operations of $81.3 million when excluding stock-based compensation and related employer payroll taxes - The company had 337 customers as of September 30, 2022, and has built lasting and significant relationships with some of the world's leading government institutions and companies, with an average revenue of $47.7 million for the top twenty customers - The company intends to expand its reach in both commercial and government sectors, with 56% revenue from government customers and 44% from commercial customers in the last nine months - The company generated 62% of its revenue from US customers in the last nine months, and expects US customers to continue to be a source of significant revenue growth for them. - The company has expanded access to its platforms to early- and growth-stage companies, anticipating that their reach will accelerate among an increasingly broad set of customers in both commercial and government sectors.
