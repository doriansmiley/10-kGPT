Rating: Negative, Score: Below Average The information disclosed in the filing suggests that the company has a multi-class structure with certain provisions that are novel or not common among other corporations with multiple class structures. Founders and their affiliates can transfer shares of Class A and Class B common stock without diminishing their voting control as long as they collectively hold 100 million Corporation Equity Securities on the record date. In addition, the Ownership Threshold is currently a small minority of outstanding Corporation Equity Securities, and future issuances of Corporation Equity Securities may decrease this percentage, making it easier for Founders to maintain effective control over the company despite diminishing their economic interest. All of these factors could potentially lead to concerns about insufficient protection of shareholder rights and dilution of minority shareholder control. Therefore, the overall evaluation score for a potential investor is below average.
