The company's sales efforts are costly, and the sales cycle is often long and unpredictable, which may fluctuate the company's results of operations. The company is subject to several risks, including dependence on a limited number of customers, fluctuations in quarterly results and seasonality, complex platforms and implementation process, failure to develop and deploy new technologies, intense competition, government regulations, intellectual property rights claims, bugs in the platforms, among others.
