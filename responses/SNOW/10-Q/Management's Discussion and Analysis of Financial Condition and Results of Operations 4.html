Management's Discussion and Analysis of Financial Condition and Results of Operations 4

Source: https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1640147/000164014722000100/snow-20221031.htm

Rating: Positive, Score: Strong Overall, Snowflake Inc. has reported an increase in its revenue and gross profit for both the three and nine months ended October 31, 2022, as compared to the same periods in the prior year. The increase in revenue was primarily due to increased consumption by existing customers, as well as the expansion of professional services. The product gross margin remained strong at 72% for each of the three and nine months ended October 31, 2022. While the cost of revenue increased, it was primarily driven by an increase in third-party cloud infrastructure expenses due to increased customer consumption. The overall increase in operating expenses was primarily due to additional equity awards granted to current and new employees, partially offset by a decrease in stock-based compensation associated with restricted stock unit awards granted prior to the IPO. Snowflake Inc. has a net loss, but this is not unexpected for a high-growth company, and the loss has decreased since the prior year period. Overall, the positive revenue growth, strong gross margin, and continued expansion of professional services indicate a strong performance for Snowflake Inc. Thus, the score is strong for an average high growth company.
